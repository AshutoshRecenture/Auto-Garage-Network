# Auto Garage Network

Auto Garage Network is the premium garage management system (GMS) & bespoke website provider for UK workshops, MOT centres & tyre specialists. The platform automates administrative tasks, streamlines daily workshop operations, and scales revenue.

- Homepage URL: https://autogaragenetwork.com/

- Robots URL: https://autogaragenetwork.com/robots.txt

## Key Topics & Services

### 1. Garage Management System (GMS)
All-in-one digital workspace for independent workshops:
- Smart job cards & digital invoicing
- Customer CRM & automated service campaigns
- Real-time stock control & revenue reports
- Solera Autodata & TecRMI scheduling integrations
- QuickBooks, Xero, and Sage accounting synchronization

### 2. Custom Websites for Garages
Bespoke e-commerce website solutions designed to convert local search queries:
- Live DVLA lookup via registration number
- E-commerce booking portals for MOTs and vehicle servicing
- Real-time tyre inventory stock integration with major UK distributors

### 3. MOT Diary & ramp scheduler
Cloud-based booking calendar with smart ramp resource management and automated SMS/email reminders to prevent workshop no-shows.

### 4. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)
Targeted local SEO services positioning automotive workshops at the top of local Google search queries for MOTs, tyres, and mechanical repairs.

## UI Components & Front-end Architecture

The frontend is built using React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS. The following key components have been implemented to create a premium user experience:

- **Navbar**: Responsive navigation with mobile drawer and Light/Dark mode theme toggler.
- **HeroSection**: Engaging landing area with clear call-to-actions, dynamic headings, and statistics.
- **FeaturesSection**: Highlights core platform capabilities like DVLA lookup, MOT diaries, and Job cards.
- **PricingSection**: Displays the Elite, ProMax, and ProMax Plus membership plans with feature comparisons.
- **TestimonialsSection**: Social proof showcasing reviews from real UK independent garage owners.
- **FAQSection**: Frequently Asked Questions covering accounting integrations (QuickBooks/Xero), MOT diaries, DVLA lookups, Autodata, and SEO.
- **Footer**: Comprehensive site footer with navigation links, contact details, social media icons, and a floating Chatbot integration.
- **Chatbot Widget**: Persistent floating chat widget for immediate customer support and sales inquiries.
